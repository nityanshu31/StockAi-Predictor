# 📈 AI-Based Stock Market Predictor & Portfolio Optimizer  

**AI-powered stock predictions, smart investment recommendations, and risk assessment**  

---

## 🚀 **Overview**  
The **AI-Based Stock Market Predictor & Portfolio Optimizer** is a web application that leverages **Machine Learning & AI** to analyze stock market trends, predict future stock prices, and optimize investment portfolios.  

It provides:  
✔ **Real-time stock tracking** 📊  
✔ **AI-powered price predictions** 🤖  
✔ **Portfolio risk & volatility assessment** ⚠️  
✔ **Automated buy/sell recommendations** 💰  

---

## 🏗 **Tech Stack**  

### **Frontend (UI & Visualization)**
- **React.js** (with Vite for faster builds) ⚛️  
- **Tailwind CSS** (for modern UI components) 🎨  
- **Recharts.js** (for interactive charts & data visualization) 📊  

### **Backend (Data Processing & AI Models)**
- **Python (Flask)** (for REST API development) 🐍  
- **Machine Learning & AI Models** (for stock price prediction) 🤖  
- **Binance API** (for live stock price tracking) 💹  

 

### **Algorithms Used**
- **Sliding Window Algorithm** – Tracks short-term stock fluctuations  
- **Heap (Priority Queue)** – Prioritizes top-performing stocks  
- **Graph-Based Correlation Analysis** – Identifies relationships between different stocks  
- **Greedy Algorithm** – Optimizes investment distribution for maximum profit  

---

## 🔥 **Key Features**  

### **1️⃣ AI-Powered Stock Price Prediction**  
- Uses **Machine Learning models** to predict stock price trends.  
- Provides **automated buy/sell recommendations** based on price movement.  

### **2️⃣ Portfolio Optimization & Risk Assessment**  
- **AI-driven portfolio analysis** for better investment strategies.  
- **Risk categorization (High/Medium/Low)** for each stock.  

### **3️⃣ Real-Time Stock Data Tracking**  
- Fetches **live market data** from the **Binance API**.  
- Displays **price movements, percentage changes**, and **volatility levels**.  

### **4️⃣ Interactive Data Visualization**  
- **Stock Price Trends** 📈  
- **Portfolio Growth Graphs** 📊  
- **Future Predictions (Next 7 Days)** 🔮
